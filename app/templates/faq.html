Getting Started

Creating your first idea is simple! Click the "New Idea" button in the sidebar or on the dashboard. Fill in the title (required), add a description, tags, and set a status. You can start with "Draft" and update it later as your idea develops. Don't forget to use tags to make your ideas searchable!

Each status represents a different stage in your content creation workflow:

  • Draft: Initial ideas that need development
  • To Film: Ready-to-create content with full planning
  • Published: Completed and live content

Tags are your best friend for organization! Separate multiple tags with commas when creating or editing ideas. Good tagging strategies include:

  • Content type: video, blog, podcast, social-post
  • Topic: fitness, cooking, tech, lifestyle
  • Target audience: beginner, advanced, teens, professionals
  • Format: tutorial, review, tips, story

Features

Our AI suggestion feature uses Google's Gemini AI to generate creative content ideas based on your input tags. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter topics or tags you're interested in
  2. Our AI analyzes current trends and your niche
  3. Multiple creative suggestions are generated
  4. Select and save the ideas you like to your dashboard

The AI considers trending topics, audience engagement patterns, and creative combinations to provide unique and relevant suggestions.

The editorial calendar helps you visualize and plan your content strategy:

  • See all your ideas plotted by creation date
  • Color-coded by status for quick overview
  • Click on any idea to view details or edit
  • Filter by status to focus on specific content stages
  • Switch between month, week, and day views
  • Plan your content publishing schedule

ContentHub supports multiple import/export formats:

Export Options:

  • CSV Export: Download all your ideas as a CSV file
  • Markdown Export: Export individual ideas as .md files

Import Requirements:

  • CSV files with headers: Title, Description, Tags, Status
  • Title field is required, others are optional
  • Use commas to separate multiple tags
  • Valid status values: Draft, To Film, Published

Troubleshooting

If AI suggestions aren't generating, try these steps:

  • Make sure you've entered tags in the input field
  • Try different or more specific keywords
  • Use English tags for best results
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page and attempting again
  • If the problem persists, the AI service might be temporarily unavailable

If your calendar is missing ideas, check these potential issues:

  • You might have a status filter active - click "All" to see everything
  • Ideas are displayed based on their creation date
  • Try switching to different calendar views (month/week/day)
  • Refresh the page to reload calendar data
  • Make sure you're logged in to the correct account

CSV import issues are usually due to formatting problems:

  • Ensure your file has the correct headers: Title, Description, Tags, Status
  • Save your file as a proper CSV format (not Excel .xlsx)
  • Check that the file size isn't too large (under 10MB recommended)
  • Make sure there are no special characters in the filename
  • Verify that text containing commas is wrapped in quotes
  • Use UTF-8 encoding when saving your CSV file

If the mobile interface isn't displaying correctly:

  • Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache
  • Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
  • Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  • Try accessing the site in incognito/private browsing mode
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Update your browser to the latest version

Account & Security

Yes, your data security and privacy are our priorities:

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely
  • Your ideas are only visible to your account
  • We don't share your content with third parties
  • Secure user authentication protects your account
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

  • All your content ideas will be permanently deleted

Tip: Before deleting, export your ideas to CSV to keep a local backup of your creative work.

Best Practices & Tips

Here are proven strategies for organizing your content:

  • Use consistent tagging: Develop a tagging system and stick to it (e.g., platform, topic, audience)
  • Update status regularly: Move ideas through Draft → To Film → Published as you work
  • Be descriptive: Write detailed descriptions to remember your vision later
  • Batch similar content: Group related ideas with common tags for efficient creation
  • Regular reviews: Weekly check-ins to update statuses and add new ideas
  • Use the calendar: Plan your content schedule using the editorial calendar view

Maximize your AI suggestions with these tips:

  • Be specific: Instead of "fitness," try "home workout routines for beginners"
  • Combine niches: Mix topics like "productivity tips for creatives"
  • Include your format: Add "video tutorial" or "blog post" to get format-specific ideas
  • Try seasonal themes: Include "summer," "back-to-school," or current trends
  • Experiment with different angles: Same topic from different perspectives
  • Save and refine: Use AI suggestions as starting points, then personalize them

Regular backups protect your creative work. We recommend:

  • Monthly exports: Download CSV backup at the end of each month
  • Before major updates: Export data before making bulk changes
  • Project milestones: Backup when completing major content batches
  • Cloud storage: Store backups in Google Drive, Dropbox, or similar services

Pro tip: Set a monthly calendar reminder to export your ideas. It only takes a minute but protects months of creative work.
